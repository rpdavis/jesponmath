## Overview
This document maps the math goals used in the app to corresponding Delta Math lessons. Delta Math offers comprehensive math instruction aligned with Common Core standards, making it an excellent supplement for students working on IEP goals.

**Note**: Delta Math's complete lesson list is not publicly accessible via API. This mapping is based on:
- The math goal templates in the app
- Delta Math's known curriculum coverage
- Common Core standard alignments

**Recommended Approach:** Use `DELTA_MATH_STANDARDS_MAPPING.md` for standards-based searching, or use this document for topic-based reference.

## Math Goal ‚Üí Delta Math Lesson Mappings

### 1. Equations

#### One-Step Equations
**App Goal**: "Solving One-Step Equations" (6.EE.7)
- **Delta Math Topics**: 
  - One-Step Equations (Addition/Subtraction)
  - One-Step Equations (Multiplication/Division)
  - Solving One-Step Equations with Integers
- **Grade Level**: 6-7
- **Example Goal**: "When given a one-step equation (e.g., 2x = 12), student will solve for the variable using inverse operations"

**App Goal**: "Solving Two-Step Equations" (6.EE.7)

  - Two-Step Equations with Fractions/Decimals
- **Grade Level**: 6-7
- **Example Goal**: "When given a two-step equation (e.g., 3x - 4 = 11), student will solve for the variable"

#### Multi-Step Equations

  - Equations with Distributive Property
  - Equations with Variables on Both Sides

- **Grade Level**: 7-8
- **Example Goal**: "Given a multi-step linear equation with rational coefficients, student will solve for the variable"

## Delta Math Topics by Grade Level

### Grade 4-5 Focus:
- Basic operations (multi-digit)
- Fractions (introduction, like denominators)
- Decimals (introduction)
- One-step word problems

### Grade 6 Focus:
- Operations with decimals and fractions
- One-step and two-step equations
- Rational numbers
- Two-step word problems
- Algebraic expressions

### Grade 7 Focus:
- Multi-step equations
- Operations with rational numbers
- Complex fraction operations
- Multi-step word problems
- Properties of operations

### Grade 8+ Focus:
- Advanced equations
- Systems of equations
- Advanced algebra topics
